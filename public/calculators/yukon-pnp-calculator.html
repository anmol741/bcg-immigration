Do you have a valid full-time permanent job offer from a Yukon employer? *
A permanent (indeterminate), full-time (30+ hrs/wk), year-round job offer from an eligible Yukon employer is mandatory for all YNP streams. Only the employer submits applications — candidates cannot apply directly.

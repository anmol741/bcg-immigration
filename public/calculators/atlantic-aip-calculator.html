Atlantic Immigration Program (AIP) 2026

The AIP is a federal program for all 4 Atlantic provinces — Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, PEI, and Newfoundland & Labrador. No CRS score required. Check your eligibility below.

📄Employer Job OfferRequired
Do you have a job offer from a designated AIP employer? *
The employer must be designated by their provincial government to use AIP. Not all employers qualify. The offer must be permanent, full-time, non-seasonal, and in an eligible occupation. Temporary or contract positions do not qualify.

💵Settlement FundsRequired
Do you have sufficient settlement funds?
AIP requires proof of settlement funds unless you are already legally working in Canada. Minimum amounts: 1 person = ~$13,757 CAD; 2 people = ~$17,127; 4 people = ~$26,788. Amounts adjusted annually.
